# About                    
                           
## Organization            

The HADACA3-datacamp challenge is strongly inspired by the HADACA3 challenge that occurred in 2024, which was organized in close collaboration between the **M4DI project, an axis of the PEPR santé numérique**, and the **ITMO Cancer Aviesan (2019-2024, project ACACIA)**. More information here: https://hadaca3.sciencesconf.org/

We are very grateful for the help provided by Nicolas Homberg during the entire setup of the challenge for this year's datacamp.
